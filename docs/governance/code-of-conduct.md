# Code of Conduct

Unconditional is a project built around care, dignity, and responsibility. The behavior of contributors must reflect the same values we expect the system to embody.

## Our Commitment

We commit to creating a community where contributors:

* Feel respected and valued
* Can collaborate without fear of harassment
* Are treated with patience, kindness, and fairness
* Can raise concerns without retaliation

## Expected Behavior

* Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* Be respectful of different viewpoints and experiences.
* Keep discussions calm, focused, and grounded.
* Accept constructive feedback with openness.
* Prioritize safety, ethics, and user dignity in all contributions.
* Default to clarity and honesty.
* Help maintain a collaborative and steady environment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Dismissive or aggressive communication.
* Contributions that knowingly introduce unsafe behavior.
* Manipulative or coercive comments.
* Any attempt to pressure others emotionally or personally.
* Derogatory remarks toward users or vulnerable populations.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior:

1. Document what happened.
2. Contact the maintainer at **[joshszep@gmail.com](mailto:joshszep@gmail.com)**.
3. Include links or evidence if possible.

All reports will be handled with seriousness, discretion, and respect.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, removal of contributions, or removal from the project. Severe or repeated misconduct may result in a permanent ban.

## Final Note

Unconditional is a project built to support people during their hardest moments. Contributors must embody the same values of compassion, clarity, and responsibility.

By contributing, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.
