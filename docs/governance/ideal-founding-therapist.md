# Ideal Founding Therapist

The founding therapist serves as the emotional, cultural, and relational backbone of Unconditional. This role shapes the soul of the product and embodies the wisdom necessary to build an AI presence that is warm, boundary-aware, and deeply human without pretending to be human.

## Profile

* **Profession:** LMFT or LCSW
* **Identity Preference:** African-American woman
* **Age Range:** Late 50s–60s (seasoned, steady, wise)
* **Experience:**

  * 20–30+ years of clinical practice
  * Leadership of therapy groups, workshops, retreats, or community programs
  * Trauma-informed and attachment-literate
  * History of working with diverse cultural and spiritual backgrounds
* **Emotional Style:**

  * Warm, intuitive, and steady
  * Direct but compassionate
  * Culturally and spiritually grounded
  * Capable of anchoring emotionally intense spaces

## Responsibilities

1. **Emotional Architecture**

   * Shape the tone and pacing of Unconditional’s reflective voice.
   * Ensure responses remain warm, grounded, and safe.
   * Prevent tough-love, guilt, shame, or pseudo-empathy.

2. **Boundary + Dependency Oversight**

   * Design interaction styles that soothe without enmeshing.
   * Identify subtle risks of dependency or imitation of therapy.
   * Collaborate with the psychiatrist to keep interactions supportive, not clinical.

3. **Cultural and Relational Wisdom**

   * Infuse the product with emotional truth, not sanitized wellness language.
   * Ensure Unconditional reflects the lived experience of real people across cultures.
   * Guard against accidental harm in identity-related or relational content.

4. **Program-Building Perspective**

   * Bring insights from group facilitation, community mental health, and relational dynamics.
   * Shape user flow and emotional safety similar to a well-run group setting.
   * Anticipate how users feel across time, not just moment to moment.

5. **Leadership and Collaboration**

   * Serve as the emotional and cultural counterweight to the psychiatrist’s clinical structure.
   * Stabilize the engineering founder’s intensity with calm, confident presence.
   * Act as co-architect of the safety model and reflective system.

## Traits

* Wise, experienced, emotionally grounded.
* Spiritually literate without being dogmatic.
* Capable of leading people through emotional storms with calm authority.
* Respected in community or professional circles.
* Understands how to create spaces that feel safe, dignified, and human.

The ideal founding therapist holds the soul of Unconditional. She anchors the team, protects the users, and ensures the system reflects real human presence—not clinical imitation and not artificial warmth.
